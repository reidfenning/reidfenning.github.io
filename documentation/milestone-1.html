Website Goals

Purpose

A webpage to represent my professional profile as a developer

Audience

Potential employers, other developers and professional peers

Goal

To acquire full or part time employment and to serve as a gallery to promote my work.

User Persona - Target Audience

Name: Billy Butcher

Technology:

  • Devices: Acer Windows 10 Laptop, Google Pixel 4 Android
  • Proficiency: Has a very strong grasp on computers and other technology as a result of building his company
  • Passion for technology: Casual

Attitudes and Needs:

  • Billy Butcher is a small business owner looking to hire a web developer to work on his website.

Frustrations:

  • Not all potential employees on github
  • Often time hard to search for a developer and immediately see their work gallery

Role:

Casual User

User Story

As a casual user, I need to quickly view and process a portfolio page so evaluate potential employees.
