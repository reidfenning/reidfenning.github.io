Milestone 2 Content Strategy

Milestone-1

My site will be one page and will consist of the following pages:

  • Welcome

    A short and sweet introduction of my Name and my potential role in Web Development. I don't want to say too much because I have all the other sections to elaborate. I will have my LinkedIn, Github and Twitter (subject to change) in the navbar.

  • About Me

    A casual paragraph explaining who I am and what my intention is in the field. I want to go over my professional history and relevant coursework. I want to mentioned where I am from and where I currently live. I want to mention that I can work solo or as part of a team.

  • Experience

    An unordered list of relevant former occupations and coursework in bootcamp. I will include my college degree and possibly my team sports history.

  • Portfolio

    This is where I will showcase the projects I have worked on thus far. I will continue to add projects as I complete them.

  • Contact

    This will be my final sections where I will have a contact form. I will have my LinkedIn, GitHub and Twitter linked a second time.
